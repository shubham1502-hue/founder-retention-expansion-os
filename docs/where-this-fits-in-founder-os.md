# Where This Fits in Founder OS

Founder Retention Expansion OS is the post-activation retention, renewal, expansion, and customer proof layer.

It starts after a customer has reached activation or initial value.

## Portfolio architecture

- AI leverage decisions: [founder-ai-workflow-roi-os](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/founder-ai-workflow-roi-os)
- Pre-call GTM: [ai-gtm-command-center](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/ai-gtm-command-center)
- Post-call sales intelligence: [founder-led-sales-call-os](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/founder-led-sales-call-os)
- Weekly revenue diagnosis: [founder-os-revenue-engine](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/founder-os-revenue-engine)
- Post-sale onboarding: [founder-customer-onboarding-os](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/founder-customer-onboarding-os)
- Post-activation retention and expansion: [founder-retention-expansion-os](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/founder-retention-expansion-os)
- Weekly operating review: [founder-weekly-operating-review-agent](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/founder-weekly-operating-review-agent)
- Investor narrative: [board-pack-investor-update-agent](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/board-pack-investor-update-agent)
- RevOps backbone: [revops-infrastructure-playbook](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/revops-infrastructure-playbook)

- Umbrella: [founder-os](https://github.com/shubham1502-hue/founder-os)

## What this repo does not duplicate

- It does not replace `founder-customer-onboarding-os`, which handles activation before customers are fully live.
- It does not replace `founder-os-revenue-engine`, which diagnoses funnel and revenue leakage.
- It does not replace `founder-weekly-operating-review-agent`, which runs the company-wide weekly review.
- It does not replace `board-pack-investor-update-agent`, which turns operating signal into board or investor narrative.

## Practical handoff

Use onboarding outputs to decide when an account has reached activation. Once activated, move the account into this repo for health, renewal, expansion, churn driver, and proof tracking.
