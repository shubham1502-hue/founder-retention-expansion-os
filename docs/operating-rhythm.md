# Operating Rhythm

Use this repo as a weekly retention and expansion review.

## Weekly cadence

Monday:

- Open `outputs/founder_retention_memo.md`.
- Review customer health snapshot.
- Confirm accounts that need founder or executive touch.

Tuesday:

- Open `outputs/renewal_risk_queue.csv`.
- Assign save plans for renewal risks.
- Confirm owner, customer sponsor, risk driver, and due date.

Wednesday:

- Open `outputs/expansion_opportunity_queue.csv`.
- Decide which accounts move into expansion discovery, proposal, or value proof.

Thursday:

- Open `outputs/churn_driver_summary.csv`.
- Convert recurring churn drivers into product, success, finance, or RevOps actions.

Friday:

- Open `outputs/customer_proof_opportunities.csv`.
- Decide which customers can become references, case studies, or champions.
- Roll major risks into weekly operating review or board narrative if needed.

## Meeting agenda

1. What changed in customer health?
2. Which renewals are at risk?
3. Which customers need founder touch?
4. Which accounts are ready for expansion?
5. Which product gaps are creating churn risk?
6. Which customers can be used as proof?
7. What owner-backed actions are due in the next 7 days?

## Decision rules

- Do not discuss expansion for unhealthy accounts until the health issue is addressed.
- Do not wait for renewal week to build value proof.
- Founder touch should be reserved for high-value risk, executive sponsor gaps, urgent churn signals, or high-leverage expansion moments.
