## Recommended source systems

- CRM for account owner, contract value, renewal date, and plan type
- Customer success tracker for health status and next step
- Product analytics for usage and adoption
- Support system for open tickets
- Finance tracker for payment status
- Founder notes for executive sponsor and relationship context

## Data hygiene

Use consistent labels. For example, use `current`, `overdue`, or `unpaid` for payment status instead of several variations. Consistent labels make scoring easier to trust.
